Understanding Leverage

Leverage allows you to control a larger position with a smaller amount of capital. For instance, with 5x leverage, you can control $5,000 worth of assets with just $1,000 of your own money. This magnifies potential returns but also increases risk.

We use leverage strategically to amplify returns while managing risk effectively. By leveraging capital responsibly, we aim to achieve substantial profits while minimizing exposure to potential losses.

Learn more about our approach to leverage and how we manage risk effectively.

Robust Risk Management

Our risk management system is designed to protect your investment while maximizing potential returns. Key elements include:

  • Margin Allocation: We allocate only 10% of the total pot as the margin for trades. This margin covers potential losses, protecting the remaining funds.
  • Stop-Loss Orders: Stop-loss orders are used to automatically exit trades at a predetermined level, minimizing losses if the market moves against us.
  • Take-Profit Orders: Take-profit orders secure gains by automatically closing trades when a target price is reached, ensuring profits are realized.
  • Diversification: We diversify investments across different assets and strategies to spread risk and reduce the impact of any single trade.
  • Continuous Monitoring: Our team continuously monitors market conditions and adjusts strategies as needed to maintain effective risk management.

Our comprehensive risk management approach ensures that while we seek high returns, your investment is protected against significant losses. Explore our trading strategies to see how these practices are integrated into our overall approach.
